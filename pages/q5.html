Font Sizes

Absolute Lengths

  • Pixels(px): Equivalent to 1px = 1/96th of 1 in
  • Points(pt): Equivalent to 1pt = 1/72nd of 1 in
  • Inches(in): Equivalent to 1in = 2.54cm = 96px

Relative Lengths

  • Rem (rem): Font size of the root element.
  • View Height (vh): 1% of the viewport's height.
  • View Width (vw): 1% of the viewport's width.
